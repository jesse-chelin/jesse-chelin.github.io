05 // The Detail

Professional Experience

Product Lead, Payments  ·  Zeller
Jun 2021 – Present
Melbourne, VIC

End-to-end product ownership for Zeller's core payments and POS software ecosystem — spanning hardware terminals, web dashboard, mobile app, and developer SDKs — serving thousands of Australian SMBs. Operating in a lean startup environment with no dedicated BAs or research teams, I own the full product lifecycle: discovery, requirements, technical design collaboration, stakeholder management, delivery, and post-launch iteration.

Product & Revenue Impact
  • Launched Zeller POS Lite from zero to 1,000+ merchants and $15.4M in GPV, delivering a fully-integrated POS across dedicated terminal hardware and the Zeller App on merchants' own devices.
  • Directed integration strategy across 1,700+ merchants and 100 POS systems via Linkly middleware, direct SDK, and cloud API partnerships — generating $1.5B in lifetime GPV.
  • Led the product strategy for Tap to Pay (CPOC) on iOS and Android — the first Australian-born fintech to offer contactless smartphone payments, with average transaction values 108% higher than traditional EFTPOS.
  • Drove delivery of Least Cost Routing (LCR) to optimise scheme routing, processing $10M+ monthly GPV through the lowest-cost channels.
  • Delivered Pay at Table / Bill at Table for hospitality (Tevalis, H&L, Impos), accelerating table turnover by up to 73%.
Reliability & Regulatory Compliance
  • Championed and drove delivery of Stand-In Processing for offline transaction continuity, retaining $186K+ in GPV during outages.
  • Drove per-scheme fee and dynamic surcharging compliance aligned with RBA Standard No. 3.
  • Identified and prioritised On-Screen Signature Verification to recover transactions from international cards without chip.
Platform & User Experience
  • Drove unification of cross-platform experiences across onboarding, invoicing, and reporting — delivering feature parity across terminal, web, and mobile.
  • Aligned Zeller Invoices with Items 2.0, with 75%+ of invoices paid within 24 hours.
  • Delivered Transaction Details and Report Summary uplifts in direct response to merchant feedback.
  • Shipped Virtual Terminal and Pay by Link for card-not-present transactions.
